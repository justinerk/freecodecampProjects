Noam Chomsky

  • 1928 - Born in Philidelphia
  • 1945 - Attended the University of Pennsylvania
  • 1951-1955 - Research at Harvard University
  • 1957 - Published Syntactic Structures and began developing his theory on generative grammar
  • 1962 - Joined antiwar protests against the Vietnam War
  • 1988 - Publishes Universal Grammar, which has been the basis of American linguistics since
    • Present - Still active politically and intellectually
